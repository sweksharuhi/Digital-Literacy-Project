Social Media Responsibility Checklist for College Students

DOs:
- Think before you post; consider how it may be perceived by others.
- Maintain professionalism, especially when connected with faculty or employers.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Respect others’ opinions and engage in constructive discussions.

DON’Ts:
- Do not post offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
- Avoid oversharing personal details like location, phone number, or financial information.
- Do not engage in online arguments or harassment.
- Avoid sharing unverified news or rumors.
- Do not neglect your digital footprint; remember posts can be permanent.
